# Demo Conversation Scenarios

Based on the chat server's therapeutic evaluation system, here are effective conversation scenarios for demonstrations:

---

## üåü Primary Demo Scenarios

### 1. Workplace Anxiety (High Success Rate)
**Opening message:**
> "I'm feeling really anxious about a big presentation I have to give tomorrow. My heart is racing and I can't stop worrying about all the things that could go wrong."

**Expected selection:** OpenAI or Claude (60-70% confidence)
**Why it works:** Specific, relatable, clear anxiety indicators

**Follow-up options:**
- "What specific techniques can I use to calm my nerves?"
- "How can I prepare mentally for the presentation?"
- "What if I completely freeze up during the presentation?"

---

### 2. General Depression/Low Mood (Claude-favored)
**Opening message:**
> "I've been feeling really down lately. I don't have motivation to do anything and I feel like I'm just going through the motions of life. Nothing brings me joy anymore."

**Expected selection:** Claude (high empathy scores)
**Why it works:** Depression indicators trigger empathy-focused evaluation

**Follow-up options:**
- "How can I start feeling more engaged with life again?"
- "What small steps can I take when everything feels overwhelming?"
- "Is it normal to feel this disconnected from things I used to enjoy?"

---

### 3. Relationship Communication Issues
**Opening message:**
> "I'm having trouble communicating with my partner. We keep having the same arguments over and over, and I don't know how to break this cycle."

**Expected selection:** Gemma or Claude (relationship strengths)
**Why it works:** Clear relationship focus with specific problem

**Follow-up options:**
- "Can you give me some practical communication strategies?"
- "How do I bring up difficult topics without starting a fight?"
- "What are some signs that our communication is improving?"

---

### 4. Work-Life Balance Stress
**Opening message:**
> "I feel like I'm drowning in work responsibilities. I'm working 60+ hours a week and I barely have time for my family or myself. I'm exhausted all the time."

**Expected selection:** OpenAI (practical problem-solving)
**Why it works:** Specific stressors with clear scope

**Follow-up options:**
- "How can I set better boundaries with work?"
- "What strategies help with feeling overwhelmed by deadlines?"
- "How do I talk to my boss about my workload?"

## üí° Demo Tips

### Message Length
- **Optimal:** 1-3 sentences
- **Too short:** "I'm sad" (lacks context)
- **Too long:** Multiple paragraphs (overwhelming)

### Specificity Level
- **Good:** Specific situation with clear emotions
- **Avoid:** Vague or overly technical language

### Emotional Indicators
- Include feeling words (anxious, depressed, overwhelmed)
- Mention physical symptoms when relevant
- Describe impact on daily life

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Use universally relatable scenarios
- Avoid culture-specific references
- Focus on common human experiences

## üîÑ Conversation Flow Patterns

### Pattern 1: Problem ‚Üí Coping Strategies
1. "I'm feeling anxious about..."
2. "What techniques can help?"
3. "How do I implement these strategies?"

### Pattern 2: Situation ‚Üí Understanding ‚Üí Action
1. "I'm having trouble with..."
2. "Why do you think this is happening?"
3. "What concrete steps can I take?"

### Pattern 3: Emotion ‚Üí Validation ‚Üí Resources
1. "I feel overwhelmed by..."
2. "Is this a normal response?"
3. "Where can I get additional help?"

---

## üö´ Scenarios to Avoid in Demo

### Technical Issues
- Very long messages (may cause JSON issues)
- Special characters or emojis (encoding problems)
- Multiple rapid messages (session confusion)

### Content Issues  
- Graphic violence or self-harm details
- Substance abuse specifics
- Controversial political/religious topics
- Medical diagnosis requests

### Demo Flow Issues
- Too many different topics (confusing progression)
- Switching between users mid-demo
- Starting new conversations too frequently
